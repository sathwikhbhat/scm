About SCM

SCM (Smart Contact Manager) is a secure, user-friendly web application designed to help you manage your contacts efficiently. Built with Spring Boot and Thymeleaf, SCM offers seamless contact management, profile customization, and robust authentication features.

Contact Management

Add, view, edit, and delete contacts with ease.

Secure & Private

Your data is protected with robust authentication and encryption.

Modern Responsive Design

Enjoy a beautiful experience on any device, light or dark mode.
